\begin{problem}{Derivative}
What is the definition of the derivative $f'(x)$?
\end{problem}

\begin{solution}
It means the rate of change of function $f(x)$.

\begin{equation}
\frac{df(x)}{dx} = \text{lim}_{\Delta{x}\rightarrow0}\frac{f(x+\Delta{x})-f(x)}{\Delta{x}} \nonumber
\end{equation}
